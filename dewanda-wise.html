Jurassic World Dominion Actress DeWanda Wise Wiki | Bio

DeWanda Wise (born on May 30, 1984) is 38 years old Jessup-born actress who is rising into prominence after landing the role of Kayla Watts in “Jurassic World: Dominion,” which got released on June 10. This new installment of the Jurassic World series involves the adventures of a unique atmosphere where dinosaurs live among humans and hunt them.

Colin Trevorrow did a fantastic job in portraying an adventurous story with the help of actors like Chris Pratt, Bryce Dallas Howard, Sam Neill, Laura Dern, Isabella Sermon, etc. If you plan to watch any movie, you can put this on your list.

This American artist is very famous because she has worked on some top-rated TV shows like “Underground,” “Shots Fired,” “Invasion,” and “The Twilight Zone.”

Early Life

On May 30, 1984, DeWanda Wise (age 38) took birth at the house of her father, John Jackson, and mother, Margie Wise, at Jessup, Maryland, United States. She spent her entire life in Jessup, and there, she, along with her brother Dwayne attended Atholton High School.

During school time, she dreamed about becoming a therapist, but later her mind changed, and she decided that acting was what she wanted to do for the rest of her life. In high school, she was when her acting coach Nathan Rosen allowed her to act in a production.

In 2002 she joined NYC’s famous Tisch School of the Arts, and from there, she completed her BFA in drama & urban studies. While at Tisch, she met Gina Rodriguez, who became a good friend of her.

Career

DeWanda’s journey as an artist began in 2006 with a short movie titled “Banished: How Whites Drove Blacks Out of Town in America,” She performed as a voice artist. It did not take much time for Wise to get a role in a significant project. In 2007, she landed her first acting role in a TV series named “Law & Order: Criminal Intent” as Angela.

From 2007 to 2016, all of Wise’s roles were either uncredited or minor. Some of the notable mentions of her work till 2016 include “Sorta My Thing,” “The Mentalist,” “Firelight,” “Boardwalk Empire,” “Tokens the Series,” “Law & Order: Special Victims Unit,” “The Good Wife” and “The Unusuals.”

After 11 years of struggle, in 2017, DeWanda got to play a lead role in a TV series titled “She’s Gotta Have It,” which ran from 2017 to 2019. Sources say that she was also selected to play a role in “Captain Marvel,” but she left the opportunity because the timing of shooting was clashing with one of her other projects.

Many people might not remember, but Wise was also in Kevin Hart’s 2021 film “Fatherhood,” where she got to play Swan. The movie was released on the occasion of father’s day, and it remained on Netflix’s Top 10 list for a long time.

Personal Life

While working on a project, DeWanda met an actor named Alano Miller and fell in love with him. After three months of their relationship, Alano proposed to Wise and asked to marry him. The actress agreed, and they tied the knot in 2009.

Both actors make one of the most handsome couples in the Hollywood industry. We never heard Wise’s name in any controversy because the lady prefers to keep herself out of such unnecessary drama. She is leading healthy personal life, and her relationship with Alano is stronger than ever.

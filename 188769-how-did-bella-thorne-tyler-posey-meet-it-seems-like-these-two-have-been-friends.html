How Did Bella Thorne &amp; Tyler Posey Meet? It Seems Like These Two Have Been Friends For A While

In the middle of August 2016, one of Hollywood's hottest young couples Bella Thorne and Gregg Sulkin split after a year of dating. But Thorne fans needn't worry that she was lonely, because about a month later, reports surfaced that Thorne was dating Teen Wolf star Tyler Posey, and they have been pretty inseparable ever since. So, how did Thorne and Posey meet?

Soon after cozy photos of them surfaced, their relationship seemed to evolve very fast, which could be attributed to a previous friendship... or just a good old fashioned whirlwind romance. As it turns out, the reason these two looked so close, so fast was that they had been friends for at least a year before they started dating romantically... and there could be some drama involved.

An interesting thing to note is that Posey is friends with Sulkin, a relationship that seemed to start they both starred in the 2012 film White Frog together. Just a few months before she began dating Sulkin, Thorne was linked to Posey in April 2015, but they disregarded the news as simply rumors and were nothing more than friends. Because Sulkin and Thorne were also friends before they dated, it might be logical to assume that they had all met throughout the years and that Thorne was friends with both of them.

Thorne and Posey were photographed walking arm-in-arm in late April 2015, kicking off the first rumors that they were dating. E! reported that the two had just presented together at the MTV Upfronts, where they were both supporting their recent projects. Thorne took to her Twitter to dispel the rumors right away:

It sounds like she was actually very serious about being upfront with her dating life, because ever since they did actually start dating, Thorne and Posey have been pretty open about being together.

Now, as to how exactly these two met, it looks like they definitely ran in the same circles. When Sulkin and Thorne were confirmed to be dating back in August 2015, a source told People that they had "known each other for years and spend a lot of time together." So, if Thorne knew Sulkin for many years, it seems probably that she also met Posey throughout that time, since Sulkin and Posey were friends. And, you know, the whole young Hollywood world can be pretty tight knit. When those rumors and pap photos of Posey and Thorne first came out in early 2015, they looked to already have a close, established friendship.

One of Sulkin's tweets in the few weeks after his breakup seemed like it could allude to his negative feelings about Posey and Thorne, according to Hollywood Life, so they might not be as close friends as they all once were. While the exact moment of their meeting isn't known, Posey and Thorne have been friends for a while and seem to have made the transition from friends to lovers really fast. And, well, that's young love for you.

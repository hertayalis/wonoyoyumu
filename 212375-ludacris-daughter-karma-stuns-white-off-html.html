Ludacris' Daughter Karma Stuns in White Off-Shoulder Top and Ripped Pants in a Photo

Ludacris' eldest daughter Karma shares a new picture of herself in her bedroom, hoping to spread light and love amid uncertain times.

Karma Christine Bridges is all grown up, and she recently decided to spread some positive vibes on Instagram by sharing a mirror selfie. Dressed in a white off-shoulder top and ripped jeans, the teenager flashed a smile as she snapped the photo.

While she decided to share the picture to spread "light and love," she also added a lengthy statement about being a black woman. In the statement which she copied from someone, she shares how important it is not to tear down members of the Black community.

AN UPLIFTING CHALLENGE

It turns out, the picture she shared was a challenge that hoped to build members of the Black community up especially during these challenging times. The challenge hoped to spark positivity and encourage others to do the same.

"We felt the pain for way too long and we have decided that we will be deliberate about building others up!!"

OFF TO COLLEGE

Ludacris' eldest is all set for college, choosing to pursue further studies at Spelman College in the fall. Karma's godparent shared the news on Instagram, sharing a picture of the teenager wearing a Spelman shirt while carrying dozens of blue balloons.

Karma is Ludacris' daughter with Atlanta-based attorney Christine White, and through the years, they've peacefully co-parented with one another. Making sure to give their daughter the best of everything, they prioritized her well-being above everything else.

HER TIGHT-KNIT FAMILY

Through the years, the proud dad has shared bits and pieces of Karma's life on social media, and it's clear how the two share a tight-knit bond as father-and-daughter. While she has not pursued music professionally, she also has a knack for it just like her dad, previously opening for Diggy Simmons when she was 10-years-old.

Rapper Ludacris and Eudoxie Mbouguiengue attend the premiere of Warner Bros. Pictures' 'Justice League' at Dolby Theatre on November 13, 2017 | Photo: Getty Images

Aside from Karma, Ludacris has a daughter named Cai Bella with longtime friend Tamika Fuller, and a daughter named Cadence Gaelle with his wife Eudoxie. The proud dad would often credit his first-born for setting a good example to her younger siblings, which is why he provides her with the best of everything as it's what she deserves.

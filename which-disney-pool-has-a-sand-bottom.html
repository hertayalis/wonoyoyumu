Which Disney pool has a sand bottom?

If you’re looking for a unique and relaxing experience at Walt Disney World, you might be wondering which Disney pool has a sand bottom. The answer to this question is the Stormalong Bay pool at Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts. This incredible pool features a sandy bottom that will make you feel like you’re at the beach, even though you’re in the middle of a theme park.

FAQs about Disney’s Sand Bottom Pool

Is the sand bottom pool at Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts real?

Yes, the sand bottom pool at Stormalong Bay is indeed real! The pool is designed to mimic a beachfront experience, complete with a sandy bottom and gentle waves. It’s the perfect place to relax and soak up the Florida sun.

Can I visit the sand bottom pool if I’m not staying at these resorts?

Unfortunately, the sand bottom pool at Stormalong Bay is only available to guests staying at Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts. However, if you’re staying at another Disney resort, there are plenty of other amazing pools to enjoy during your stay.

Are there any other pools at Disney World with unique features?

While the sand bottom pool at Stormalong Bay is certainly one of the most unique, there are other pools at Disney World with special features. For example, the pool at Disney’s Art of Animation Resort has underwater speakers that allow you to listen to music while you swim.

Is the sand at the bottom of the pool real?

Yes, the sand at the bottom of the pool is real! The pool is designed to create a beach-like experience, and the sand is an important part of that. It’s just one of the many details that Disney Imagineers have included to make the pool feel like a true oasis.

How deep is the sand bottom pool at Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts?

The sand bottom pool at Stormalong Bay is relatively shallow, with a maximum depth of around 5 feet. This makes it the perfect place for both kids and adults to swim and play in the sand.

Are there any special events or activities at the sand bottom pool?

Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts often host special events and activities at the sand bottom pool. From poolside games to movie nights, there’s always something fun happening at Stormalong Bay.

What other amenities are available at Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts?

In addition to the amazing sand bottom pool, Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts offer a wide range of amenities for guests to enjoy. These include world-class dining options, a spa, and even a marina where you can rent boats and other watercraft.

Can I rent a cabana at the sand bottom pool?

Yes, guests staying at Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts have the option to rent a private cabana at the sand bottom pool. This is a great way to enjoy some extra luxury and privacy during your day at the pool.

Are there any age restrictions for the sand bottom pool?

The sand bottom pool at Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts is open to guests of all ages. However, children under the age of 14 must be accompanied by an adult while at the pool.

What are the pool hours at Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts?

The sand bottom pool at Stormalong Bay is typically open from early morning until late evening, allowing guests to enjoy plenty of time in the sun and sand.

Can I bring food and drinks to the sand bottom pool?

While outside food and drinks are not allowed at the sand bottom pool, there are plenty of dining options nearby where you can grab a snack or a refreshing beverage.

What is the best time of year to visit the sand bottom pool?

The sand bottom pool at Disney’s Yacht and Beach Club Resorts is a great place to visit year-round. However, if you’re looking to beat the crowds and enjoy milder temperatures, consider visiting in the fall or spring.

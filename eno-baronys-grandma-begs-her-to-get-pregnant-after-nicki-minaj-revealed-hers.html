Eno Baronys Grandma Begs Her To Get Pregnant After Nicki Minaj Revealed Hers

Rapper, Eno Barony has released a WhatsApp voice note of his grandmother begging her to get pregnant after hearing Nicki Minaj is pregnant.

The female rapper cracked ribs with the recording video-tape and social media folks have reacted.

The video posted by Eno Barony, the grandmother who’s name is yet to be known was heard begging his granddaughter to take a seed and give her a child.

According to the grandmother, she stated that the rapper should also focus on getting a baby and halt a bit on the money-making.

The old lady seem to be quite worried about the situation and begged her to grant her that wish.

She captioned the post that “After my grandma heard Nicki Minaj is pregnant… I don’t know whether she thinks she is a Ghanaian or we are competing or sumfn she gave me tym at the end”.

MUSIC: Kizz Daniel Lie

Award winning sensational Nigerian singer and energetic performer, Kizz Daniel breaks his long time silence with a new beautiful song titled; “Lie”.

The new song by the Flyboi Inc leader happens to be his second record of the year, 2021. “Lie” comes after his track, ‘Currently’ featuring YBNL Nation leader, Olamide and rapper, Falz.

‘Lie’ owes production, mixing and mastering credit to popular Nigerian record producers, Philkeyz and Blaise Beatz.

Kizz Daniel, officially called Oluwatobiloba Daniel Anidugbe graduated with a degree in Water Resources Management and Agrometeorology at the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta. He is well known for his singles “Woju” and “Yeba”.

ALSO: Magnom ft Caine – My Taste

‘Lie’ is a great piece of music you will want to add to your playlist and play endlessly. Take a listen below.

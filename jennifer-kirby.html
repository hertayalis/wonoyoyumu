Vampire Academy Actress Jennifer Kirby Wiki | Bio

33-years-old Jennifer Kirby (born on August 18, 1988) is an English theatre and television actress. She is well known for playing Nurse Valerie Dyer in the BBC One historical drama “Call the Midwife.” Jennifer began appearing on the program in the series’ sixth season and continued to portray Valerie until the show’s ninth season. The lady is also a performer at the Royal Shakespeare Company.

How Old is Jennifer Kirby?

Jennifer Ann Kirby (age 33), better known today as Jennifer Kirby, took birth on August 18, 1988, to her mother, Née Coulson, and a businessman father. She was raised with her younger sister Eleanor in the Worcestershire village of Longdon.

While living in Worcestershire, Jen went to Malvern St. James Girls School in Malvern Hills, and after completing her schooling, she studied English Literature and Drama at the University of East Anglia and Graduated from the same institute in the year 2010.

To become an artist, Ann enrolled in the elite London academy of music and dramatic art (LAMDA), where notable actors like Benedict Cumberbatch and Jim Broadbent also studied after studying English literature at the University of East Anglia in Norwich. From there, Jennifer started getting roles.

Acting Career

Elizabeth Bennet was Kirby’s first significant acting role, and she performed it at Regent’s Park Open Air Theatre in London. For this performance, she received nominations for Outstanding Newcomer at the 2013 Evening Standard Theatre Awards and for London Newcomer of the Year at 2014 What Onstage Awards.

In prominent roles, she has also been on stage in “The Recruiting Officer” at the Salisbury Playhouse and Teddy at the Southwark Playhouse. For her 2014 performances as Lady Percy in Henry IV, Parts 1 and 2 at the Royal Shakespeare Company, as well as her roles as Katherine in Henry V for the RSC, she got a Commendation at the  Ian Charleson Awards.

She made her television debut in an episode of the famous BBC One medical drama Holby City in 2015, and in 2017 she made her debut in Call the Midwife’s sixth season as Valerie Dyer, an ex-Army nurse. She acknowledged leaving her position on Call the Midwife after four years in 2020. She had a guest appearance in episode 3 of season 8 of the police drama Endeavor in 2021 as Dr. Gillian Nicholls.

Who is Nam Joo-hyuk?

Nam Joo-hyuk is a South Korean actor and model best known for portraying Han Yi-an in the KBS2 drama series Who Are You: School 2015? He has walked the runway for labels such as SONGZIO Homme as a model.

He has appeared in films such as Who Are You: School 2015 (2015), Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-Joo (2016), Moon Lovers (2016), The Bride of Habaek (2017), The Light in Your Eyes (2019), The School Nurse Files (2020), and Start Up (2020). Nam’s excellent acting and modeling career earned him a spot on Forbes’ 30 under 30 lists in 2020.

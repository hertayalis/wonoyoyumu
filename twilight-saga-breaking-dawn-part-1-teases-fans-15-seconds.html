The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn Part 1 Teases Fans For 15 Seconds

Recommended Videos

With The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 1 rapidly approaching its theatrical debut on November 18, 2011, Summit Entertainment has released a new teaser trailer. This release follows the recent trend of building anticipation for the trailer itself, and gives fans another 15 seconds of their favorite characters.

The first trailer premiered at the 2011 Movie Awards just a few weeks back. It showcased the marriage of Edward Cullen (Robert Pattinson) and Bella Swan (Kristen Stewart), one of the movie’s biggest plot points.

This new teaser starts off where the first trailer left off. This 15-second trailer highlights the supernatural pregnancy of Bella with her half-human, half-vampire offspring– another huge plot point.

Along with the new teaser, two promotional posters were released. The first one of Edward and Bella as the happy couple; and the second one of Jacob (Taylor Lautner) and his wolf pack.
